📅 Last updated: June 2025 · 🌐 Boleiros Copa 2026
🛡️
Short version: Boleiros Copa 2026 does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

01Overview

Boleiros Copa 2026 ("the App", "we", "our") is a free football management game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is designed to be clear and transparent about our data practices.

02Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to us
  • Any information that could identify you personally

03Local Data Storage

The App saves your game progress (save slots, team choices, match results) exclusively on your own device using local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is fully controlled by you — you can delete it at any time by clearing the app data in your device settings
  • Contains no personal information — only game state data

The App functions completely offline. No internet connection is required to play, and no data is sent or received during gameplay.

04Social Sharing

The App includes an optional feature to share game results to social media or messaging apps. When you use this feature:

  • An image is generated on your device only
  • We do not receive or store the shared content
  • Sharing is initiated entirely by you, using your device's native share sheet
  • Any data shared with third-party platforms (WhatsApp, Instagram, etc.) is governed by their own privacy policies

05Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Facebook SDK or Meta Pixel
  • Advertising networks of any kind
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data

06Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

07Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

08Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? We're happy to help.

02Informações que Coletamos

Não coletamos nenhuma informação pessoal. Especificamente, o App não coleta:

  • Nome, endereço de e-mail ou qualquer informação de contato

O App não coleta dados de nenhum usuário, incluindo crianças menores de 13 anos. Como nenhuma informação pessoal é coletada, o App está em conformidade com o COPPA, LGPD e regulamentações similares.
